This Privacy Policy describes how FaceLoom ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

  • Email address (when you create an account)
  • Authentication credentials
  • User ID generated by our system

1.2 Face Scan Data

  • Facial images you choose to scan
  • Face mesh data and analysis results
  • Scan timestamps and metadata

1.3 Usage Information

  • App usage statistics and analytics
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Crash reports and error logs

1.4 Purchase Information

  • Subscription status and purchase history
  • Payment processing is handled by Apple/Google and we don't store payment details

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and improving our face analysis services
  • Personalizing your app experience
  • Processing your subscription and managing premium features
  • Sending important updates and notifications
  • Analyzing app performance and fixing bugs
  • Ensuring security and preventing fraud

3. Data Storage and Security

We take data security seriously:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/SSL
  • Data is stored securely using Supabase infrastructure
  • Face scan data is stored with strong encryption
  • We implement industry-standard security measures
  • Access to your data is strictly controlled and monitored

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

4.1 Supabase

Database and authentication services. Data is stored on secure servers.

4.2 RevenueCat

Subscription management and in-app purchase processing.

4.3 MediaPipe (Google)

Face detection and mesh generation. Processing happens locally on your device.

5. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained while your account is active
  • Face scan data is stored until you delete it or close your account
  • Analytics data may be retained for up to 2 years
  • You can request deletion of your data at any time

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent at any time

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
